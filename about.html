The First Meet

February 18, 2023

Mumbai’s Design scene was down bad. But this was the pivotal moment when things changed for the better. Rishabh & Sooraj met for the first time and had an inspiring conversation about the future of Mumbai’s Design Scene at Starbucks, Thane. With a solid roadmap for the future and a shared vision to bring Mumbai back to the design map in India, FOF Mumbai was revived this day

Config Watch Party 2023

June 24, 2023

Our very first event, and what a way to kick things off! The Config Watch Party became an instant hit and marked the revival of FOF Mumbai, setting the stage for a new chapter in the design history of Mumbai. The success of this event truly set the bar for future endeavors

A Brand New Team

August 24, 2023

Realizing that organizing high-quality events was no small feat, Sooraj & Rishabh decided to bring in fresh talent. A team that could take our vision to the next level. After sifting through hundreds of applications, conducting countless interviews, we handpicked a group of the most passionate and skilled individuals. The new team injected a burst of energy into FOF Mumbai

Intro to Variables Workshop

September 11, 2023

Our very first virtual event, the Intro to Variables Workshop, saw over 200 designers joining from across the world to learn how to use variables in Figma. Hosted by Sooraj, the event included interactive games, virtual speed networking, and much more, making it an engaging and memorable experience for everyone involved

Design x Dev Meetup

October 28, 2023

Our second in-person event and our first major collaboration with the developer community via GDG MAD. It was a groundbreaking moment for us as it marked our first foray into cross-community collaboration. The event hosted at Fractal, brought together designers and developers in a way that set the tone for future collaborative initiatives

Wireframed 2023

December 10, 2023

Our flagship annual conference, Wireframed 1.0, was an absolute milestone for FOF Mumbai. The event featured an incredible lineup of thought leaders and industry experts, making it an unforgettable experience for 130+ attendees. The success of this conference demonstrated our ability to put on high-caliber events and made us immensely proud

Summer Design Meetup

March 17, 2024

To kick off the new year, we hosted the Summer Design Meetup, featuring top-notch speakers who sparked thought-provoking discussions. This event, hosted at Pineapple Studios was primarily hosted for folks who had never attended our events before. It was a great way to reconnect with the design community after a busy year, set new goals, and share fresh ideas

GDSC Techfest 2024

April 21, 2024

We were community partners for the very first GDSC Techfest which brought together all the Google Student Developer Clubs across Mumbai. Sooraj & Rishabh were invited as speakers for the event and gave a presentation on how to start with Figma and how they grew this community organically from the grounds up

Designwings Designathon

May 12, 2024

We were community partners for the Designwings Designathon hosted by Punit Chawla. Rishabh & Sooraj both had the honor of judging the event, which showcased brilliant talent from across the design community. The event was a huge success and a fantastic learning experience for everyone involved

MeetJam #1 - Thane

May 18, 2024

We introduced ‘MeetJam’ - a fresh new event series focussed on networking, fun and creativity. 20-25 people come together to engage in fun activities like wireframing challenges, curated networking and brainstorming sessions. The aim was to create a space for designers to connect and collaborate in a relaxed atmosphere. The first MeetJam happened at Starbucks, Thane - yes the very spot where FOF Mumbai was revived

1000+ WhatsApp Community Members

June 11, 2024

Our WhatsApp community has been the heart of FOF Mumbai, where members share knowledge, have fun, and support each other. We were thrilled to celebrate reaching over 1,000 community members – a milestone that shows just how strong and connected our community has become

FOF Mumbai Becomes #1 in India

June 28, 2024

A moment of immense pride – FOF Mumbai is now the largest official Figma community in India and among the top 5 globally! This achievement reflects the hard work and dedication of our members, team, leaders, speakers, and partners. We’re deeply grateful to everyone who has been part of this journey and helped us reach this incredible milestone

Config Watch Party 2024

June 29, 2024

The second edition of Config Watch Party brought even more excitement. Hosted back at our favorite venue, Fractal, the event saw new additions such as interactive presentations, board games, polaroid photo booths, and more. It was an even bigger success than the first, with a growing number of designers and creatives from various industries coming together

Design Vichaar 2024

July 20 - 21, 2024

One of our largest events to date, Design Vichaar 2024, was a massive collaboration with Frelo (formerly Creto) and Somaiya School of Design. Over two action-packed days, we hosted 250+ attendees with workshops on Day 1 and fireside chats and presentations on Day 2. The event brought together designers, innovators, and thought leaders to discuss the future of design in an engaging and hands-on manner

LottieFiles Certification Workshop

August 24, 2024

In partnership with LottieFiles, we hosted a highly successful online workshop attended by over 200+ designers from across the world. The workshop was centered around creating motion designs using the new Figma-to-Lottie plugin, with participants also earning certification. It was an excellent opportunity for designers to learn cutting-edge skills while networking with like-minded professionals

Farewell... Team 1.0

September 4, 2024

Here’s to Team 1.0: the dreamers, doers, and trailblazers who laid the foundation of FOF Mumbai—your legacy will always inspire us

Figma Workshop for ISTE KJSCE

September 13, 2024

Conducting an in-person Figma workshop had always been on our radar since the beginning and that became a possibility with this collaboration with the awesome folks at ISTE KJSCE. Sooraj did a intro presentation on what UX Design is and how to get started with Figma with a hands-on workshop

Team 2.0

October 7, 2024

As we continue to grow, some of our original team members moved on to new opportunities, but with change comes new energy. Team 2.0 was born and expanded to 10 folks – bringing fresh perspectives and new expertise to keep FOF Mumbai evolving and pushing boundaries

MeetJam #2 - Andheri

October 20, 2024

Building on the success of the first one, MeetJam 2 happened at Doolally, Andheri and took our casual networking series to the next level. Once again, designers gathered to connect, collaborate, and enjoy activities that helped break down barriers. With an ever-growing community, it became an event designers look forward to as a creative outlet and a way to meet new faces in the industry

Wireframed 2024

December 7, 2024

Surprise! Surprise! We did it again – and this time, we brought even more to the table. Wireframed 2.0 was a bigger, bolder version of our flagship conference. With an even more stellar lineup, interactive sessions, and bigger ideas, this event cemented our place as one of the premier design communities in Mumbai and beyond

Farewell, Rishabh...

31 December, 2024

Rishabh Singh, one of our founding members and Community Lead since January 2022, officially stepped down from his role on December 31, 2024. From helping us host our very first event to shaping FOF Mumbai into one of the most recognized design communities in India, Rishabh’s journey has been nothing short of inspiring. His dedication and passion for building this community have left an indelible mark on all of us.

A New Era

January 01, 2025

As we enter 2025, we’re excited to share an update for Friends of Figma, Mumbai Meet our new Co-Lead: Kunal Thakkar! Since joining us in August 2023 as our Social Media Manager, Kunal has gone above and beyond to contribute to our community’s growth. Whether it was leading campaigns, assisting with tech and logistics, or lending a helping hand wherever needed, his dedication and proactive approach have made a huge impact.

MeetJam #3 - Navi Mumbai

January 25, 2025

MeetJam is back, and this time we're heading to Navi Mumbai! No speakers, no agenda—just designers coming together for networking, food, and games. After exploring Thane on the Central Line and Andheri on the Western, it’s time to connect with our Harbour Line community.
