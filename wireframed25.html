FAQs

What is Wireframed? +

Wireframed is FOF Mumbai’s annual flagship event. The format changes every year, as per community demand and for 2025, it transforms into a 24-hour offline designathon.
Who can participate? +

Working professionals of all experience levels are invited to participate.
I’m a student, can I participate in Wireframed 2025? +

Sadly, not this time. Wireframed 2025 is open only to working professionals - that means participants who have graduated from college and are currently working in the design or tech industry.

We totally understand that many students are already doing amazing work through internships or part-time roles - but to keep this edition fair and balanced, we’re limiting participation to professionals for now.

Don’t worry though - we’re already planning a student-focused event next year so that you get your own space to collaborate, learn, and shine.

Stay tuned for updates on that, and we’d love to have you join us then!
Is there a registration fee? +

No. All FOF Mumbai events, including this designathon, are free of charge to our attendees and members.
Can I participate individually? +

This is a team-based competition. Individual participation is not permitted. If you are seeking team members, please join our WhatsApp Wireframed Team Search group here.
Are the problem statements different for each round? +

Yes - the online and offline rounds feature unique problem statements.
I am not based in Mumbai. Can I still participate? +

Absolutely! Participants from across India can register. Travel and accommodation for the offline finale will be self-arranged.
Do I need to be an FOF Mumbai Member? +

Yes. Membership is free — sign up at friends.figma.com/mumbai.
What is the scoring matrix for the Designathon? +

Scoring Matrix:
• Problem Understanding
• Solution Relevance
• User–Centered Design
• Visual Design & Craft
• Use of AI Tools / Processes
• Communication, Collaboration & Presentation
What are the sponsorship benefits? +

Partnering with us comes with powerful visibility and community impact. Whether you’re a new brand looking to make a mark or an established one aiming to champion design, innovation, and startups — sponsorship helps you do both.

Expect your brand to shine through:
• Prominent logo placements
• Inclusion in event goodie bags
• Dedicated social media features
• Opportunities for giveaways or promotions

Interested in collaborating? Reach us at this form.
Do you have a Code of Conduct? +

We’re committed to creating a safe, inclusive, and welcoming space for everyone — both at our events and online. We celebrate diversity and expect all participants to act with respect, empathy, and professionalism.

By joining our events, you agree to uphold these shared values. Further details can be found at figma.com/legal/code-of-conduct.
