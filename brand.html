Dos and Don'ts

Rotate logo 15° or -15° if needed.

Give the logo space against visuals.

Mumbai name should always be easy to read.

Use logo in white wherever applicable

Don't remove outer circle.

Don't use secondary or tertiary logo without prior approval.

Never use the “F” alone.

Never use heart alone.

Don’t change the font or adjust spacing.

Don’t change the colors. Don’t color in the “F”.
