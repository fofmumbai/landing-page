MeetJam #5 - Bandra

We’re bringing MeetJam back for its 5th edition - this time - as highly requested - in Bandra! ​Expect a cozy afternoon of meaningful conversations, fun group activities, and curated networking with the FOF Mumbai design community.

Register
MeetJam

No speakers, no agenda—just designers hanging out! MeetJam is all about exploring Mumbai, trying new places, and playing games in a casual setting. A small crowd keeps things cozy, and the cost of food or activities is split among attendees.

The Basics:

  • Each table has an organiser to guide and maintain the event flow
  • Our media team will be capturing the event via photos/videos. Say Hi!
  • For F&B, it'll be a bill split by table at the end of the event
  • The starter (chaat) and the dessert (cookies) are on us :)

Don't shy away from clicking loads of photos and tag us on Instagram @fof.mumbai
